Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2024

Introduction

Inner Sweetness ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Your name and email address, provided during account creation or other interactions.
  • Usage Data: Information such as page views, listening metrics, and activity data.
  • Device Information: Data collected from your device, including your phone number.

2. How We Collect Data

  • Sign-Up Forms: Information you provide directly when registering or interacting with the App.
  • Firebase Analytics: To track usage data and improve functionality.
  • Sentry: To monitor and resolve technical issues effectively.

3. Purpose of Collecting Data

  • Usage Metrics: To maintain service quality and improve the App.
  • Personal Information: To personalize your experience and ensure service quality.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes. Data collected is used solely to improve the quality of our services and maintain App functionality.

5. Accessing, Updating, and Deleting Your Data

Access and Updates: You can access and update your information directly through the App.

Deleting Your Account: To delete your account and associated data, send a request to hello+deleteme@innersweetness.org. If there is no outstanding balance, we will process your request within 30 days.

6. Security Measures

  • Encryption: All server communications are encrypted using TLS (Transport Layer Security).
  • Database Security: Data is encrypted and backed up regularly using industry best practices.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access data for essential job functions.
  • Monitoring: Systems are monitored for vulnerabilities and unauthorized access.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at: hello+privacy@innersweetness.org.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App, and the "Effective Date" will reflect the changes.
